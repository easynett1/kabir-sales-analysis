# Kabir Retail Solutions

## Regional Sales Performance Audit Report

---

## Executive Summary

Sales are decreasing over time, showing a downward trend from 2010 to 2012. This indicates that overall business health is declining with reduced revenue generation each year.

---

## Efficiency Analysis
The Sales Rep with the highest transaction count but not the highest revenue is Abdul.
    - Abdul handled a high number of transactions but generated less revenue compared to top earners.
    - This suggests that Abdul focuses on volume sales i.e. many small transactions rather than high value deals.
    - In contrast, top performers generate more revenue with fewer or more valuable transactions.


## Insight

The business may benefit from training sales rep like Abdul to upsell or target higher value customers and improving overall revenue efficiency.

### Trend Analysis (Yearly Performance)

•	2010		: 			₦104,720 (Highest)
•	2011		: 			₦83,600
•	2012		: 			₦80,960
There is a consistent decline in revenue over the years.

### Recommendation

The company should NOT hire more staff in 2013. Instead, it should consider reducing or optimizing staff and improving sales strategy.


## Reasoning
•	Declining sales indicate reduced demand or inefficiencies
•	Hiring more staff during a downturn may increase costs without increasing revenue
•	Focus should be on:
        o	Improving sales performance
        o	Training staff
        o	Enhancing marketing or product value
